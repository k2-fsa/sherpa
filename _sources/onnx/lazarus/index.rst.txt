Lazarus
=======

We also provide examples for developing with `<https://www.lazarus-ide.org/>`_
using `Object Pascal <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Object_Pascal>`_.

We provide support for the following platforms and architectures:

  - Linux-x64
  - Windows-x64
  - macOS-x64
  - macOS-arm64
